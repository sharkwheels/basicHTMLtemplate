The Deets

Punk Prism Power v1.0 was made for the DMG Killer Interfaces jam in Feb 2015. It runs on Unity, and uses some rad tiny Arduinos called Light BLue Beans.

OMG this is AWESOME! When can I play it?!

Its currently in basic prototype stage, but we'll be working on getting v2.0 up and going over this year, and looking to bring it to events. If you want to keep up to date, you can find us on twitter: @punkprismpower.
